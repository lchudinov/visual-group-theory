## 2.6 Exercises

## 2.6.1 Basics

**Exercise 2.1.** In the rectangle puzzle, what actions were the generators? What other
actions are there besides the generators?

Actions were a horizontal flip and vertical flip. Other actions: any combination of the flips.

**Exercise 2.2.** In the light switch puzzle, what actions were the generators? What other
actions are there besides the generators?

Actions were a flip of switch 1 and flip of switch 2. 

**Exercise 2.3.** Can an arrow in a Cayley diagram ever connect a node to itself?

Yes, it is an identity action.
