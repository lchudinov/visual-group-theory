# The Triangle Puzzle

`e` - identity
`r` - A clockwise 120 degree rotation
`r^2` - A clockwise 240 degree rotation
`f` - A horizontal flip
`rf` - rotate + horizontal flip
`r^2f` - rotate twice + horizontal flip

`D_3 = <r,f>`

`r^3 =e` `r^(-1) = r^2` `f^(-1) = f` `rf = fr^2` `r^2f = fr`

Non-abelian: `rf != fr`
